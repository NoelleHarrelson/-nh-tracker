02What's Different in SFR

Emotion drives more than you're used to.

In multifamily, it's 100% numbers. In SFR — especially primary residences — buyers fall in love first and rationalize second. Your role is to help them fall in love with the right property and confirm the numbers support the decision.

California disclosure is your due diligence equivalent.

CA requires extensive seller disclosures — Natural Hazard Disclosure, TDS, flood zone, local ordinances. Read every disclosure, know what it means, protect your buyers. Being the agent who flags issues early builds instant trust.

The timeline is compressed — but your discipline isn't.

MF deals took months. SFR escrow is typically 21–30 days. Your PE discipline around process, deadlines, and documentation is a real advantage. Most agents miss dates. You won't.

Coastal = California Coastal Commission.

Any renovation or addition near the coast falls under the California Coastal Commission — affecting permitting timelines, setback requirements, and what can be built. Being the agent who surfaces it early is a major differentiator.
