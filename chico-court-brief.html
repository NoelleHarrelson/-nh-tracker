The Street
Chico Court is one of Silver Strand's most unique addresses — a rare street running parallel to the beach rather than perpendicular, tucked into its own quiet nook between the water and the harbor. The lot configuration at 2301 is distinctly triangular, a corner position that gives it a footprint unlike anything else on the street. Low turnover here isn't a red flag — it's a signal. People don't leave Chico Ct.
The Vibe
Silver Strand is a neighborhood you have to know to know. No drive-through traffic, no tourist strip — just coastal residents, beach bikes, and the kind of quiet that makes people stay for decades. Chico Ct sits in the heart of that. The 3-story layout means views compound with each floor — something that doesn't show up in the sqft number.

The Read
Priced 11% above a declining RVM in a market trending down 10.6% year over year — that's the headline. STR yield comes in at 5.4% (AirDNA) to 4.9% (Rabbu), below the coastal investor target of 6–8%. Both scenarios carry negative at ask with a conventional 20% down structure.

This one pencils better at $1.2M–$1.25M. At that price point gross yield crosses 6% and the carry gap closes to near breakeven. The 3-story layout, fireplace, balconies, and Silver Strand address give it lifestyle appeal — but the numbers tell a motivated seller or patient buyer story, not a slam-dunk investor play at list price. Seller is offering concessions — worth noting.
